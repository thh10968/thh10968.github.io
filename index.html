Magic: The Gathering is the worlds most popular Trading Card game. Released in 1993 by Wizards of the Coast, Magic is the first trading card game and has approximately thirty-five million players as of 2018.

Magic can be played by two or more players, either in person with printed cards or online using the Magic The Gathering app. the player takes over the role of a "Planeswalker" and cast spells to try and defeat the opponent. A planeswalker has many different kinds of spells in their arsenal including creatures, instants, sorcerys, and enchantments. The are two primary ways of playing magic the gathering, standard and commander. Standard is a 2 player format with 60 card decks and each player has 20 life. Commander is Magic The Gatherings most popular format. Commander is played with up to 4 players and 100 card decks with each player having 40 health to start the game.

There are approximately 22,600 individual Magic cards with their own unique names and mechanics that are used to aid a player in defeating their opponent. New cards are released on a regular basis through expansion sets. The first exapnsion, Arabian Nights was released in 1993. New sets and revisions to the core rules have been released since.
